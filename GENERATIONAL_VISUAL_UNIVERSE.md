## 3. Visual philosophy (non-negotiable)

We are an **animation studio**, not a generic AI video factory.

**Require**
- One connected cinematic universe
- Consistent characters, lighting, silhouettes, environments
- Camera movement, depth, atmosphere, educational clarity
- Every sentence has a visual purpose
- Every new asset becomes permanent company IP

**Forbid**
- Random art styles per video
- Inconsistent character redesigns
- Static slideshows / empty / color-bed frames
- Generic disposable AI imagery with no library reuse
- Approving productions that weaken the brand
