## Mission

MacroCenter is the original futuristic research & visualization hub where every Generational educational episode begins.

Not a classroom. Not an office.  
An advanced center where knowledge becomes tangible, interactive, and alive.

## Animation language

High-energy original cartoon principles (Generational-owned):

- Fluid motion · expressive timing · clear silhouettes  
- Strong poses · readable face · elastic but controlled motion  
- Camera moves only when it teaches (establish → push-in → orbit/reveal → macro)

---

## Interaction law

The Professor does not lecture at slides.  
They **grab, rotate, expand, shrink-into, walk-around, and demonstrate**.

Audience should feel inside the lesson.

---

## Sound

- Soft futuristic hub ambience under narration  
- Subtle interface ticks on hologram beats  
- Educational underscore optional; never drown speech

## Episode contract

1. Open inside MacroCenter (establishing beat)  
2. Hook (2–3s)

4. Explanation

6. Takeaway + curiosity bridge  
7. MacroCenter remains visible as brand home
