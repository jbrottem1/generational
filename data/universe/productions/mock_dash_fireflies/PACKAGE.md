## Full Script

Fireflies don't just glow for decoration — they're sending messages. That light is chemistry: a tiny reaction in their bodies that releases energy as a flash. Different patterns can mean different signals — like a secret code in the dark. Often, it's about finding a mate. Sometimes the glow also says: I'm here… pay attention. So every flash is more than pretty — it's a conversation written in light.
