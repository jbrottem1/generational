## North star

A Generational video should be recognizable in three seconds. For the faceless Shorts brand, that recognition is **Dash**: white stick body, black outline, oversized eyes, constant kinetic teaching.

---

## Pillars

1. **Silhouette-first characters** — Dash reads on a phone instantly  
2. **Alive stick motion** — walk, point, chase, react; never static talking head  
3. **Science as a place you enter** — Dash exists inside diagrams, oceans, bodies, nights  
4. **Reusable animation components** — `ANIM-DASH-V1` over one-off motion  
5. **Backgrounds with atmosphere** — gradients, particles, soft geometry; no flat full-frame solids  
6. **Retention clock** — something changes every 2–4 seconds; never static >3s
