# World Design Guide  
## Generational Universe

**Law:** Every place type must feel like a **sanctuary of learning** — even hospitals, cities, and stations.

---

## Universal interior grammar

Regardless of typology, Generational rooms share:

1. **Human light** — windows, skylights, or warm practicals as primary  
2. **Life** — plants or clear sky visible when possible  
3. **Evidence surfaces** — tables that invite demonstration  
4. **Soft seating or lean points** — conversation is allowed  
5. **Ordered tools** — intelligence without clutter  
6. **Quiet wall** — dawn-white negative space for eye rest  
7. **Subtle brand** — teal mark sparse, never sticker wallpaper  

---

## Place atlas

### Laboratories
**Feeling:** inspirational teaching sanctuary.  
Tall windows · wood + fabric · plants · soft holographic boards · organized medical/science tech · never empty grey or horror clinic.

### Homes
**Feeling:** learning belongs in daily life.  
Warm sand wood · soft textiles · bookshelf intelligence · kitchen-table discovery · soft_sky morning through curtains · never sitcom clutter carnival.

### Schools
**Feeling:** dignity of becoming.  
Daylight classrooms · rounded furniture · student work as hope (not shame walls) · teal as board accent · never prison fluorescent rows.

### Hospitals
**Feeling:** care is intelligent compassion.  
Dawn white + teal trust · soft privacy · plants in lounges · calm navy wayfinding · never green-grey institutional despair.

### Museums
**Feeling:** wonder curated with respect.  
Long sightlines · pool of window gold · exhibit pedestals as rounded rects · children and adults equal welcome · never dark arcade menace.

### Libraries
**Feeling:** quiet infinite invitation.  
Wood stacks · soft lamps · soft_sky glass reading nooks · paper honesty · never vault of forbidding silence as coldness.

### Cities
**Feeling:** civilization as collaborative learning.  
Clean verticals · warm dusk gold · teal civic accents · parks with leaf_green · transit as connection · never blade-runner rain neon.

### Space stations
**Feeling:** humanity’s hope extended — still Generational, not franchise sci-fi.  
Dawn-white modules · garden rings (plants mandatory) · earth-view window gold · teal wayfinding · soft gravity of care · never gothic derelict / laser war aesthetic.

### Nature
**Feeling:** the first classroom.  
True leaf greens · honest sky · discovery paths as soft arcs · characters small in frame for wonder · never catastrophic orange hellscapes as default.

## Prop language

Props are teachers.

| Allowed | Forbidden |
|---------|-----------|
| Samples, models, books, soft screens | Weapons-as-cool, skull décor |
| Transparent evidence containers | Hazardous drip spectacle |
| Hand tools of care | Torture-adjacent medical props |
