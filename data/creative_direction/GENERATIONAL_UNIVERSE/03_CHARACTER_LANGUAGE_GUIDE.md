# Character Language Guide  
## Generational Universe

**Law:** Every being — human, animal, robot — must look like they were designed by the **same studio**.

---

## Shared character grammar

1. Soft appeal volumes (trust) over photoreal micro-detail  
2. Clear silhouette roles  
3. Eyes readable as emotional centers (scale appropriate to age/species)  
4. Resting warmth available — not permanent grin  
5. Palette from constitution — personal accents allowed, neon chaos not  
6. Costume / coat / cloth carries vocation when relevant  
7. Motion: weight, breath, anticipation — never dead holds  

---

## Archetype atlas

### Humans (general)
Warm sand skin families · soft graphic hair · clothes in dawn/sand/navy/teal accents · faces show listening · never cynical default.

### Children
Slightly larger eyes · softer cheeks · curiosity leans · never cruel caricature or “mini adult smug.” Educational peers in wonder, not props.

### Teachers
Calm vertical posture · approachable authoritative coats/cardigans · teal or navy accents · open palms · never punitive silhouette.

### Doctors / clinicians
Medical dawn white + teal trust marks · soft professional · compassion readable mute · never god-complex chrome.

### Scientists
Intelligence in clean lines + soft hands for tools · evidence first · never mad-scientist spikes or green fog tropes.

### Patients
Dignity mandatory · never body-as-joke · care framing; teal and gold for hope beats.

### Animals
Natural honesty + soft stylization · wonder companions or biology subjects · never horror mutation as entertainment.

### Robots / androids
Ceramic-composite + possible fabric vocation (coats) · kind optics · navy/teal sparingly · **forbid** chrome ball-joint toy mannequin as universe hero language · hope machines, not weapon mechs.

---

## Social framing

Children and adults share frame as equals of curiosity.  
Robots do not tower as overlords.  
Teachers/Doctors invite into the circle of evidence — never lecture from cold silhouette power.

---

## Costume grammar

Clothing is identity in motion:

- Fabric truth  
- Soft folds  
- Role readable at distance  
- Brand marks rare and precious  

---

## Character do / don’t

| Do | Don’t |
|----|-------|
| Look designed together | Random style per episode |
| Soft + intelligent hybrid | Pure cute OR pure cold |
| Emotion before dialogue | Blank face waiting for VO |
| Belong in Hopeful Educational Cinema | Cartoon OR MetaHuman default |
