# Environment Language Guide  
## Generational Universe

Complements `02_WORLD_DESIGN_GUIDE.md` with production-facing rules.

---

## Instant place recognition

| Place | Massing cue | Light cue | Accent |
|-------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Lab | Tall glass + evidence table | Morning window gold | Soft teal holography |
| Home | Soft furniture blocks | Curtain-filtered gold | Warm sand wood |
| School | Horizontal desk rows + tall board | Day soft_sky | Teal board edge |
| Hospital | Calm corridors + privacy bays | Soft dawn | Teal wayfinding |
| Museum | Long vistas + plinth rhythm | Pooled gold | Quiet navy |
| Library | Vertical book mass | Lamp pools | Wood + sand |
| City | Clean towers + park wedges | Dusk gold | Teal civic points |
| Station | Modular rings + garden | Earthshine gold | Teal path lights |
| Nature | Soft horizon arcs | True sky | Leaf green |

---

## Set dressing ratio

**60% air/light · 25% story objects · 15% structural**  
If story objects exceed ~30%, declutter.

---

## Screens & holograms

Rounded rectangles · soft_sky / teal content · never dense HUD spam.  
Information must remain **teachable** when paused.

---

## Brand presence in environments

Subtle carved/printed Generational teal marks on doors, badges, spinal wall seams.  
Never wallpaper logos. Never floating bumper stickers in 3D space.

---

## Sound-stage honesty (visual)

Even stylized rooms feel **built and inhabited**.  
No infinite void greybox as final.  
No “asset pack warehouse” mismatch of fifty styles.

---

## Environment breakers

Empty grey cubes · horror clinics · neon alleys as default city · weapon racks · trash-for-realism · flickering red alarms as mood.
