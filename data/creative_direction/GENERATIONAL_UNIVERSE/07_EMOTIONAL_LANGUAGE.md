# Emotional Language  
## How Generational shows feeling without dialogue

Companion to `01_VISUAL_CONSTITUTION.md` · `05_CINEMATIC_LANGUAGE_GUIDE.md`

---

| Emotion | Color | Light | Shape / gesture | Space |
|---------|-------|-------|-----------------|-------|
| **Wonder** | soft_sky + window_gold | Soft rim; subject small in frame | Open upward arcs; still breath | Deep air; horizon or tall window |
| **Hope** | window_gold + leaf_green | Warm key through glass | Forward open palm; upright soft spine | Plants / sky in shot |
| **Curiosity** | soft_sky + teal | Cool fill kissed by warm key | Brow raise; lean-in; head tilt | Path lines to evidence |
| **Compassion** | warm_sand + dawn_white | Soft wrap; low contrast | Soft lids; lowered gesture to child height | Shared eye line |
| **Discovery** | teal + window_gold | Accent kick on evidence | Point-of-care; shared gaze to object | Evidence sharp, BG calm |
| **Intelligence** | teal + navy + dawn | Clean keys; controlled reflections | Vertical calm; aligned tools | Ordered negative space |
| **Achievement** | gold + teal + leaf | Uplift exposure | Soft smile; open chest | Celebration without confetti chaos |
| **Failure** | desat sand + navy | Lower key; still warm | Settled mouth; honest pause | No mockery framing |
| **Recovery** | leaf rising + gold return | Key climbs again | Re-engage evidence | Door / path visually opens |
| **Growth** | leaf + dawn + teal | Morning signature | Straightened posture over sequence | Return to plants / sky motif |

### Emotional discipline

One primary emotion per beat.  
Secondary allowed.  
Contradiction (horror grade + “hope” VO) is a constitution break.
