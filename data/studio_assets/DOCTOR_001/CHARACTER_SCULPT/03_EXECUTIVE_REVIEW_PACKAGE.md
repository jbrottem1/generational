## Art director review (brutal)

### Silhouette — still FAIL for icon lock

Coat is wider and hem language exists. Blackout would still read **stacked spheres + egg midsection + thin sticks**, not an iconic medical coat host. Exposed shoulder/elbow balls remain silhouette noise.

### Head sculpt — still FAIL for uniqueness

Egg scale is insufficient. The audience still sees a ball with stickers (brows, mouth bar, cheek dots, ear nubs). Integrated cranial crescent is not yet a memorable profile break.

### Face — warmth incomplete

Resting smile keys exist; screen read remains near-neutral bar mouth. Kindness is not yet visible before dialogue.

### Eyes — closer, not iconic

Darker pupil hierarchy helps. Eyes still read as attached spheres / goggles rather than living sockets in a continuous face.

### Coat — best idea, unfinished delivery

Coat remains the highest-ROI path. Current delivery is still **cylinder ribbing + hard lapel boxes**, not garment. Until the coat alone is drawable as clothing, brand ownership fails.

### Body / hands

Hands still fail teaching CU. Limb capsules still shout “assembly toy.”

### Materials

Matte ceramic intent is fine. Materials cannot rescue primitive forms.

4. **Coat is volume costume, not clothing.** Ribbed egg ≠ lab coat.
5. **Emotional stillness.** Mute frames still need audio apology.

## The question

> If this version were shown beside Baymax, WALL-E, and other iconic animated characters, what still prevents it from becoming equally memorable?

### Answer

**It still lacks a single, ruthless iconic idea made of continuous form.**

Baymax is unforgettable because one soft care-volume + dark oval eyes equals “help.”  
WALL-E is unforgettable because binoculars + cube + tread equals “lonely curiosity.”  
Their shapes are destiny.

This Doctor is still a **competent pile of on-brand parts**: white shell, teal accents, egg torso, sphere joints, pasted face.  
Children will still draw **a white robot**.  
They will not yet draw **The Doctor**.

Until coat silhouette and face continuum beat the sphere stack — until one glance encodes mentor medicine without labels — Generational does not have an icon. It has a prototype wearing intentions.
