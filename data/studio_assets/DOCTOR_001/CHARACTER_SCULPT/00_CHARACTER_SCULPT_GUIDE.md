# Phase VIII — Character Sculpt Guide

**Character:** DOCTOR_001 · The Doctor  
**Thesis:** Concept C — The Inspiring Mentor  
**Studio:** Character Sculpt Studio  
**Architecture:** Frozen · Mesh / materials only · No new engines  

---

## Mission

Solve only this problem:

> The Doctor is still perceived as a white robot instead of a unique character.

This guide defines the industrial sculpt that creates recognition. It is not a scoreboard.

---

## Sculpt north star

One child-memory drawing must contain:

1. **Dawn coat with flared hem**  
2. **Teal pin + thin piping**  
3. **Soft egg head with navy cranial crescent** (integrated, not a hat)  
4. **Kind large eyes with dark pupils**  
5. **Warm resting smile**

If any of those fail in blackout or CU, the sculpt fails.

---

| Rank | Change | Recognition impact | Why first |
|------|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **1** | **Coat as logo silhouette** | Critical | Owns the “Doctor” memory; kills sphere-torso blackout |
| **2** | **Egg head + integrated cranial crescent** | Critical | Kills “ball + blue beanie robot” |

Reject anything below this list that only adds seams, bolts, or gizmos without changing the child drawing.

## 1. Silhouette sculpt

### Target outline

Tall medical coat rectangle → soft shoulder pads → flared hem → soft egg head with crescent profile → connected limbs (no gap dots).

### Changes

| Change | Why better | Memorability | Trust | Silhouette | Animation |
|--------|------------|--------------|-------|------------|-----------|
| Widen coat body + **dramatic hem flare** | Clothing volume beats torso egg | Child draws coat first | Medical educator | Primary blackout win | Hem bone follows walk |
| Soft **coat shoulder** overhang | Host not stick-figure under fabric | Shoulders of mentor | Approachable authority | Soft pads in shadow | Clavicle weights |
| Longer sleeve stubs / cuff | Arms under garment | Coat continuity | Professional | Fewer limb dots | Arm motion clearer under cloth |
| Kill exposed chrome elbow/knee spheres | Mannequin language dies | Not “robot joints” | Less toy / threat | Continuous limbs | Same bones; fairing deforms lightly |

### Rejected

Extra mech vents, floating teal wings, cape — complexity without recognition.

## 2. Head sculpt

### Target

Soft **egg** (slight overscale), not UV sphere. Cranial navy is a **crescent integrated into the skull**, not a glued hat.

| Change | Why | Memorability | Trust | Silhouette | Animation |
|--------|-----|--------------|-------|------------|-----------|
| Non-uniform egg scale (deeper Y, taller Z) | Unique cranial shape | Distinct profile | Soft intelligence | Crescent + egg readable | Head bone unchanged |
| Sink / flatten cranial into crown | Removes beanie robot | Crescent = Doctor mark | Dignity | Profile cue | Facial keys unaffected |
| Softer jaw sphere + shorter chin | Less cone / goatee | Friendly face | Warmth | Soft jaw in side view | Jaw key still binds |
| Continuous neck flare into collar | Body–head unity | Whole figure reads | Calm | Neck not floating | Neck bone deform |

### Rejected

Helmet visors, antennae, ear antennas — sci-fi costume, not educational mentor.

- Height ~1.85 m  
- Facial shape-key names preserved
