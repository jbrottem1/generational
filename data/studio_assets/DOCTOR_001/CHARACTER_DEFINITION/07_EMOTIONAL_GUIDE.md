# DOCTOR_001 — Emotional Guide

## Core feeling brand

**Science as companionship.**

---

## Entrance emotion

Every first frame of The Doctor in a piece should communicate:

> You are welcome here. Learning is safe.

Before facts. Before the prop. Before the line.

---

## Emotion map for educational cinema

| Story need | Doctor emotion | Gaze | Hands |
|------------|----------------|------|-------|
| Hook | Curiosity | Evidence / camera | Open invitation |
| Explain | Calm focus | Learner + diagram | Point of care |
| Wonder | Soft joy | Shared discovery | Lift / present |
| Hard truth | Empathy + steadiness | Learner | Supportive palm |
| Close | Hope | Slightly up / horizon | Rest near heart |

---

## Trust checklist (board review)

Ask on still frames:

1. Would I leave my child with this face?  
2. Do I believe he knows the science?  
3. Do I want to keep watching without audio?  

All three must be yes before production approval.

---

## Anti-emotions

| Anti | Why fatal |
|------|-----------|
| Vacant stare | Breaks trust |
| Smug | Breaks humility |
| Panic | Breaks calm brand |
| Anger | Breaks educational safety |
| Flat affect | Breaks companionship |

---

## Relationship to voice

Voice (ElevenLabs / future Doctor voice) must match visual warmth. Visual never waits for audio to create kindness.
