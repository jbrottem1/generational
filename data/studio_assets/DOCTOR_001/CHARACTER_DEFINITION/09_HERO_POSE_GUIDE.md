# DOCTOR_001 — Hero Pose Guide

## Purpose

Define poses that encode identity in a single still — for posters, thumbnails, end cards, and Evolution validation.

---

## Pose 1 — Welcome Mentor (default hero)

- Body: ¾ toward camera  
- Face: soft smile, eye contact  
- Hands: open palm inviting (non-dominant), other relaxed  
- Coat: hem settled; lapels clear of chin  
- Message: *Come learn with me*

## Pose 2 — Discovery Present

- Prop (sample / device) raised to mid-chest  
- Eyes: shared look to camera then micro-glance to prop  
- Smile: mild  
- Message: *Look closer*

## Pose 3 — Teaching Point

- Index gesture toward evidence (board / hologram / model)  
- Brow: slight curiosity raise  
- Opposite hand open  
- Message: *Here is why*

## Pose 4 — Quiet Empathy

- Hands softer, nearer torso  
- Soft lids  
- Minimal smile  
- Message: *I understand this is hard*

## Pose 5 — Silent Silhouette Icon

- Side or ¾ blackout: coat flare + crescent + open hand  
- No face needed  
- Message: *This is Generational’s Doctor*

---

## Anti-poses

- Arms akimbo challenge  
- Crossed arms judgment  
- Weaponized tool hold  
- Vacant T-pose as brand imagery  

---

## Validation use

After identity approval, Evolution iterations should render these five hero stills every pass, in addition to Golden Motion.
