# DOCTOR_001 — Visual Identity Guide

**Phase VII · Definition only**  
**Recommended concept:** C — The Inspiring Mentor  

---

## Brand position

The Doctor is Generational’s face of **discovery with compassion**.

He must be recognizable in:

- YouTube thumbnail (1:1 crop face or three-quarter)
- Shorts vertical (full-body entrance)
- Museum / classroom mural (silhouette)
- App icon (eyes + cranial crescent + pin simplified)

---

## Three concept directions (evaluated)

### Concept A — The Gentle Scientist

**Look:** Softest curves, oversized soft eyes, deep friendly smile, rounded coat, softer shoulders.  
**Emotional win:** Instant kindness.  
**Risk:** Reads “kids toy / soft robot”; loses clinical credibility for parents; silhouette may balloon.  
**Score (design intent):** Warmth 9 · Trust 8 · Profession 5 · Icon potential 7  

### Concept B — The Futuristic Physician

**Look:** Elegant ceramic planes, precise seam lines, narrower eyes, sharper coat tailoring, cool confidence.  
**Emotional win:** Respect and intelligence.  
**Risk:** Cold; trust delayed; can slide toward generic sci-fi clinician.  
**Score:** Warmth 5 · Trust 6 · Profession 9 · Icon potential 7  

### Concept C — The Inspiring Mentor ★ RECOMMENDED

**Look:** Soft mentor face (A) + clean coat/ceramic structure (B). Subtle cranial crescent. Resting smile. Kind focused eyes. Coat as trademark. Teal pin.  
**Emotional win:** Kindness *and* competence in one glance.  
**Risk:** If execution slips toward spheres, collapses to greybox again — must enforce integrated sculpt language.  
**Score:** Warmth 8 · Trust 9 · Profession 8 · Icon potential **9**  

### Why C

Wall-E / Baymax / Iron Giant succeed because their shapes encode a clear heart. Generational needs that heart plus classroom gravitas. C is the only direction that carries both without diluting either.

---

## Identity pillars (permanent)

1. **Cranial crescent** — navy, elegant, integrated into skull (not a beanie).  
2. **Warm sand face** with soft smile resting.  
3. **Dawn-white medical coat** with teal piping + Generational pin.  
4. **Kind teal-iris eyes** with clear pupils (no headlights).  
5. **Tall approachable coat flare** silhouette.

Remove any competing chrome joints, floating geometry, or toy sphere language from the final visual target.

---

## Don’ts (visual)

- Perfect UV sphere head  
- Balloon cheek spheres  
- Flat bar mouth  
- Detached limb capsules with metal ball joints exposed  
- Floating cyan plates as “coat”  
- Villain / laser eye emissive  
- Military plating  

---

## Do’s (visual)

- Continuous head & body forms with soft transitions  
- Readable mouth lips for speech + emotion  
- Coat that looks like clothing in blackout  
- Asymmetry in micro-expression  
- Soft specular control on ceramic  

---

## Relation to current pipeline asset

Current `production_v1` procedural mesh is a **prototype actor**, not the Bible look.  
Evolution iterations after approval must converge toward Concept C — not toward polish of kitbash spheres.
