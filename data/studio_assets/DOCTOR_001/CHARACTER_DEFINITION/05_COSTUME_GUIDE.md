# DOCTOR_001 — Costume Guide

## Philosophy

The coat is not a prop.

The coat **is** The Doctor’s logo in fabric form.

If the coat fails the clothing-alone test, the character fails.

---

## Signature coat (Concept C)

### Structure

1. **Collar** — soft stand / rolled medical collar, continuous with lapels  
2. **Lapels** — asymmetric gentle open V toward −Y teaching cameras  
3. **Body** — dawn-white; believable thickness; soft vertical fall, not egg ribbing  
4. **Hem** — flare below hips; readable in silhouette and walk follow-through  
5. **Sleeves** — full to cuff; cuff slightly darker or navy-edge optional  
6. **Shoulders** — soft pads integrated with coat (hide harsh mech joints)  
7. **Piping** — thin Generational teal along lapel/front edge only  
8. **Insignia** — Generational pin left chest; always on camera-facing plane  

### Movement

- Hem responds to walk (garment bones or controlled hybrid)  
- Lapels stay clear of face in CU  
- No torso intersection in reach/grasp  

### Tools (optional set dressing on coat)

- Small badge / ID volume (non-brand-clashing)  
- Subtle inner pocket line  
- Never weaponized gadgets  

---

## What the coat must communicate alone

- Medical ✓  
- Educational ✓  
- Generational ✓  
- Approachable ✓  

---

## Anti-patterns (current prototype debt)

- Ribbed egg reading as “torso texture” not fabric  
- Floating cyan slabs  
- Missing collar/hem  
- Pin too small or facing wrong axis  

These are rejected in the final identity.
