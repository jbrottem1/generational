# Master Concept Art — The Doctor (`DOCTOR_001` / `CHAR-0001`)

**Status:** PERMANENT visual identity lock  
**Package:** `data/studio_assets/DOCTOR_001/MASTER_CONCEPT_ART/`  
**Style target:** Premium animated feature (Pixar / Arcane / DreamWorks / Unreal cinematic)

This is **not** a temporary illustration and **not** a storyboard sketch.

These images are the official master concept art for The Doctor across the Generational Universe.

Every future image, animation, episode, educational video, and promotional asset must faithfully reference this design.

| Attribute | Spec |

| Build | Athletic, approachable, natural heroic proportions |

| Skin | White ceramic composite, soft satin, microtexture |
| Eyes | Large expressive warm cyan-blue irises, natural sclera, trustworthy glow |
| Wardrobe | Navy underlayer · white medical overcoat · cyan accents · Generational insignia |
