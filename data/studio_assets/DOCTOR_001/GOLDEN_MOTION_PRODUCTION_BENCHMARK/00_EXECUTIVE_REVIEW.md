## Executive answers

### 1. Does this finally feel like a permanent Generational character?
**No.** Identity *direction* (Living Lab Coat) is visible in silhouette mid-shots. Embodiment is still manufacturing geometry. Under performance camera stress it reads as a soft-robot prototype, not a permanent host.

### 2. Would a Pixar / Disney / DreamWorks character supervisor approve this performance?
**No.** They would approve the *blocking pass* (beats, path, camera plan, speech window) and reject the asset for lock: disconnected limbs at CU, unreadable teaching hands, non-cloth coat, weak facial topology, pedestal base. This is layout + manufacturing check, not finals.

### 3. Five most noticeable remaining weaknesses
1. **Disassembly under CU / turn** — parts float; shoulder spheres / pointed sleeves / neck / face fragments (see `04b` / `05b` evidence stills).  
2. **Hands fail gesture language** — cannot sell teaching, grasp honesty, or trust.  
3. **Coat is sculpture, not cloth** — vertical egg-pleat, no collar/lapel/cuff authority.  
4. **Face fails empathy CU** — sticker/kitbash; smile & trust do not hold hero framing.  
5. **Pedestal / disc base** — kills grounded educator stance and walk authority.
